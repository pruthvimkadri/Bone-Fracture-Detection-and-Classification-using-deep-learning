About Us

Frac-AI is an AI-powered bone fracture detection system that analyzes X-ray images using deep learning, Grad-CAM visualization, and hybrid edge detection. It helps doctors and students visualize potential fracture zones for better diagnosis.

Faster, reliable, and accurate medical imaging solutions at your fingertips.
